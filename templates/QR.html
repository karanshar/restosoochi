QR Code Generator

Implementation Methods


  1. First, select a template and download it.


  2. Open it with any image editing tool of your choice.


  3. Insert your QR Code in the empty box provided.


  4. Adjust the size of the QR Code by dragging the corner to fit the empty box slot. Always drag from the corner to maintain its original shape and do not drag from the top, bottom, or sides.


  5. Save and export the image in a high-quality print format.
